# Writer — alpha 0.2.5

Thanks for testing! Writer is an app for planning and writing stories in one
place: you zoom from the big picture down to the words.

## Installing

Run `Writer_0.2.5_x64-setup.exe`. Windows SmartScreen will warn because the
alpha isn't code-signed yet — click **More info → Run anyway**.

## Getting started

1. Click the folder icon under **Stories** and pick (or create) an empty folder
   — that's your library. Every story is a plain-markdown folder inside it.
2. **+ New story**, pick the Novel template.
3. Click panels to dive in, or **ctrl+scroll up/down** to zoom in and out —
   that's the core of the app. Notes you write at the chapter or part level are
   planning notes; they never end up in the exported manuscript.
4. Highlight a character's name in your text → right-click → **Tag as
   character**. From then on every mention anywhere is a clickable link.
5. The book icon in the left ribbon is **Preview** — your whole story compiled
   into one readable page, exactly as an export would come out. Click any
   heading in it to jump back to that section and edit.
6. Check Settings → **Hotkeys** for everything else (Ctrl+F search, Ctrl+H
   replace, drag panels to reorganize, right-click panels for status…).

## Your writing is safe

Everything is plain markdown files in your library folder — readable by any
editor, yours forever. The app also keeps automatic backups (History — the
clock icon next to the story name) and you can snapshot the whole story any
time. Nothing is ever silently deleted.

## When something breaks

- A **red banner** at the top means the app hit an error — please screenshot it
  and note what you were doing.
- If anything looks wrong, confusing, or annoying — that's exactly the feedback
  wanted. Notes on what felt *good* help too.

Known alpha limitations: default app icon, no auto-update (new versions come as
new installers), Windows only for now.
