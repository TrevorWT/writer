Hotkeys & gestures

Ctrl+F / Ctrl+PSearch the story
Ctrl+SSave now (everything autosaves anyway)
Ctrl+B / Ctrl+IBold / italicize the selected text
Ctrl+HFind & replace
Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+YUndo / redo moves, deletes, adds (text has its own undo while typing)
EscBack out one layer; closes menus and panels first
Ctrl+scroll upDive into the panel under the cursor
Ctrl+scroll downBack out one layer
Click a panelOpen it
Double-click a panel titleRename it
Right-click a panelStatus, numbering, delete
Right-click selected textTag, note, find, replace, clipboard
Drag a panelReorder; drop on a gap places it there
Alt+drop on a panelNest inside it
Drag onto outline rowsTop/bottom edge = before/after, middle = inside
Drag onto the story nameMove to top level
Scroll on the panel rowScrolls it sideways
