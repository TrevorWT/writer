# The Hollow Crown

A disgraced knight must return the crown she stole — but the kingdom that exiled her is already burning.

## Beginning

[[Mara]] lives in hiding in [[Port Vessel]] with the crown. Rumors of war reach her. Establish: her guilt, the crown's strange weight, the bounty on her head.

### Chapter 1 — The Fishmonger's Knight

Open on Mara gutting fish. A royal herald arrives looking for her. She almost turns herself in.

#### Scene: the herald at the docks

Fog. The herald reads the proclamation aloud. Mara stands ten feet away, unrecognized.

## Middle

The journey back to the capital. Mara learns the crown is keeping the old king alive — returning it might kill him.

## End

Mara gives up the crown but not to the throne — to the fire. What does she keep? Her name.
