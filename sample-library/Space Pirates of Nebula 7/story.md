A washed-up cargo pilot accidentally steals the galaxy's most wanted sandwich recipe and becomes public enemy number one.

# Beginning

# Beginning

[[Dex]] is broke, his ship [[The Rusty Pelican]] is falling apart, and he takes one last shady delivery job from [[Big Wanda]]. The package turns out to be the secret recipe. Establish: Dex's debt, his talking navigation AI [[POLLY]], and why everyone wants this recipe.

## Chapter 1 — One Last Job

Dex gets the job offer, almost says no, takes it anyway because the rent on his docking bay is due.

### Scene: the cantina meeting

Big Wanda slides a datachip across the table. "Don't open it. Don't scan it. Don't even look at it funny." Dex looks at it funny.

#### Page 1

The cantina on [[Docking Ring 9]] smelled like engine grease and regret. Dex nursed the cheapest drink on the menu and pretended he wasn't counting Big Wanda's bodyguards. There were six. There were always six.

#### Page 2

"You're late," Wanda said. "I'm consistent," Dex said. The datachip was small, black, and humming faintly, which datachips should not do.

### Scene: POLLY scans the chip anyway

POLLY ignores three direct orders and scans the chip. Alarms across forty star systems go off simultaneously.

## Chapter 2 — Everyone Shoots at Dex

Bounty hunters, the Galactic Culinary Guild, and Wanda's own crew all converge on The Rusty Pelican. Dex escapes by flying through a car wash for ships.

# Middle

On the run, Dex learns the recipe isn't a sandwich at all — it's a coded map to the lost pantry of [[Chef-Emperor Gustavo]], whose condiments could end the galactic food shortage. He teams up with [[Rix]], a disgraced food critic with a grudge and a very good palate.

## Chapter 3 — The Food Critic

Rix saves Dex from a bounty hunter using only a bottle of aged vinegar. They form an uneasy alliance: Dex wants the bounty off his head, Rix wants the pantry.

## Chapter 4 — The Trail of Crumbs

Following the map through three star systems. Each waypoint is guarded by a trial: the Soup Nebula, the Asteroid Bakery, the Gravity Well of Infinite Brunch.

### Scene: the Soup Nebula

The Pelican's hull is not rated for bisque.

# End

At the pantry, Big Wanda is already there — she had a second copy of the map. Standoff. Dex realizes the pantry's lock only opens for someone who cooks with love, which rules out literally everyone present except POLLY, who has secretly been running a recipe blog for years. POLLY opens the pantry, the food shortage ends, Dex's debts are paid in condiments, and Rix writes the review of a lifetime.

## Chapter 8 — The Pantry Door

Final confrontation. Wanda's six bodyguards vs. one vinegar-armed food critic and a sarcastic AI.
